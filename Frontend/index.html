Our Heritage

Subramaniya Sasthri Higher Secondary School has stood as a pillar of quality education in Arani for over a century. Founded with a vision to educate every child regardless of background, the school continues its mission with dedication and pride.

About Us

A Legacy of Learning
& Values

Established around 1914, S.S. Higher Secondary School is one of the oldest institutions in Tiruvannamalai district. We provide Tamil & English medium education from Class 6 to Class 12, nurturing young minds with academic rigor, cultural values, and character.

Our Facilities

Everything a Student Needs

Our campus is equipped with modern facilities to support holistic development — academic, physical, and creative.

🔬

Science Laboratory

Well-equipped labs for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology to encourage hands-on learning and scientific thinking.

💻

Computer Lab

Modern computer lab with internet access, enabling students to develop digital literacy and technical skills.

📚

Library

A rich collection of books, magazines, and study materials for students across all classes and subjects.

🏃

Sports Ground

Spacious grounds for cricket, football, kabaddi, and athletics to keep students fit and competitive.

🎭

Cultural Activities

🎖️

NCC / NSS
