About Us

A Legacy of Learning
& Values

Established around 1914, S.S. Higher Secondary School is one of the oldest institutions in Tiruvannamalai district. We provide Tamil & English medium education from Class 6 to Class 12, nurturing young minds with academic rigor, cultural values, and character.
