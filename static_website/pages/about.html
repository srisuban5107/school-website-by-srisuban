About Our School

Our Heritage

Subramaniya Sasthri Higher Secondary School has stood as a pillar of quality education in Arani for over a century. Founded with a vision to educate every child regardless of background, the school continues its mission with dedication and pride.

Government Aided State Board Co-Educational

About Us

A Legacy of Learning
& Values

Established around 1911, S.S. Higher Secondary School is one of the oldest institutions in Tiruvannamalai district. We provide Tamil & English medium education from Class 6 to Class 12, nurturing young minds with academic rigor, cultural values, and character.

Vision & Mission

🌟 Our Vision

To be a beacon of holistic education that empowers every student with knowledge, values, and the confidence to contribute meaningfully to society and the nation.

To provide quality, inclusive education in a nurturing environment — fostering academic excellence, cultural pride, and strong character in every learner from Class 6 through Class 12.
